Game Stream
GoStream is a streaming platform, in progress, developped by IMT Atlantique-Brest students, for the sake of filming, streaming, detecting and digitizing a Go Game for the Go club Tenuki in Brest, France.
The platform allows the user to precisely recognize a Go board during a live stream, detect black and white stones, assign them to their right positions, and visually reproduce the game for training purposes.
